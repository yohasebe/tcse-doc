# Pause video after segment

Check **Stop automatically** (or press `A` for Study Mode) before making a search. The video will automatically pause after each segment finishes playing, allowing you to review the content at your own pace.

![Input panel with Stop Automatically checked](images/20.png)

This is particularly useful for language study — see [Pause and Check](../using-tcse-for-language-learning-and-education/using-pause-and-check.md).
