# Fine-tune sync between video and transcript

Sometimes video and transcript are not in sync. In such cases, try the following:

1. Pause the video (otherwise you can only see closely adjacent segments)
2. Locate the segment that is expected to be played when the video is resumed
3. Click on the **adjust** button (clock icon) on that segment's row

![Adjust button (clock icon) in transcript view](images/13.png)

The adjust button appears as a gray clock icon next to each segment number in the transcript view during video playback. Clicking it corrects the offset between the video's current position and the segment's start time, bringing the video and transcript back into sync.
