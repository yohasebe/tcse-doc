# Play a segment of a talk

Click on the **play** button in the search results to open the video player.

![Play button in search results](images/01.png)

Click on the video screen to start playback at the onset of the segment. The HTML5 video player uses HLS.js for streaming.

![Video player](images/02.jpg)

## Jump to a segment by clicking

While a video is playing, you can click on any segment text in the transcript to jump directly to that point in the video. This allows you to quickly navigate to any part of the talk without using the video's seek bar.
